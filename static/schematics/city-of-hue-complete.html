Executive District

Income: Elite Security: Maximum

Home to the most powerful corporations and their senior officers. Towering headquarters pierce the smog layer, the tallest belonging to Hughes Industries. Private security patrols every block. Mansions sit behind reinforced walls.

Key Locations

  • 🏢
    Hughes Industries HQ
    Tallest structure in the city. Hughes' face projected on the south facade 24/7.
  • ⚙️
    Prometheus Corp
    Military contracting giant. Supplies weapons systems and private soldiers.
  • 🌿
    Hughes Private Gardens
    Restricted green space for senior management. Secure outdoor meeting area.
  • 🚁
    Executive Helipad
    Connects directly to Hughes Station via atmospheric shuttle twice daily.

Commonwealth District

Income: Above Average Security: Moderate

One of the more comfortable residential zones in the city. Clean streets, functioning infrastructure, and genuine green space make this a desirable address for professionals and mid-tier corporate employees.

Key Locations

  • 🌿
    The Garden
    One of the few genuine parks in the city. Maintained greenery, walking paths.
  • 📚
    Rowan Academy
    One of only three accredited private schools in Hue.
  • 🍷
    The Meridian
    Finest dining in the district. Popular for informal corporate meetings.
  • 💻
    The Tech Quarter
    Cluster of small tech firms and freelance engineers.

Central District

Income: Middle Security: Standard

The backbone of Hue's working population. Middle-income residential blocks packed tightly together, neighborhood shops, and the constant hum of urban life.

Key Locations

  • 🚊
    Central Transit Hub
    Main transit node for the city. 180,000 passengers daily.
  • 🍔
    Food Fight
    Cheapest hot food in the district. Open 24 hours.
  • 📖
    Hue Public Archive
    One of few unmonitored public data terminals in the city.
  • 🛒
    Central Market
    Sprawling daily market. Legitimate and illegal goods coexist peacefully.

Shantytown

Income: Impoverished Security: None / Gang

The poorest district in Hue. Labyrinthine streets of makeshift housing. Gang law is the only reality. Despite the poverty, a stubborn community culture persists.

Key Locations

  • 🪙
    Yamai's Pawnshop
    Run by a drow who buys everything and asks nothing.
  • 🍺
    Broken Dreams Bar
    The social center of Shantytown. Information trades here.
  • 🍲
    Mother Hen's
    Community kitchen. 300 free meals a day. Protected ground.
  • ⬇️
    The Hollow
    Well-known access point to the Underground.

Entertainment District

Income: Mixed Security: Corpo-Patrolled

Where the city spends its money and forgets its problems. Casinos, combat arenas, clubs. Every vice is for sale, every spectacle available for the right price.

Key Locations

  • 🎰
    Lucky Dwarf Casino
    Multi-floor casino with an unofficial basement sports book.
  • ⚔️
    Bloodsport Arena
    Capacity 8,000. Licensed death matches and the underground Merc Circuit.
  • 🌙
    The Afterparty
    Opens 03:00. Has been 'about to be shut down' for nine years.
  • 🔬
    Chrome & Flesh
    Grey-market augmentation parlor. Fair prices, no medical records.

Industrial District

Income: Working Class Security: Heavy (Elevator Zone)

The engine room of Hue. Factories run day and night. Most importantly, the district hosts the foot of the Space Elevator connecting the city to Hughes Station at 420km altitude.

Key Locations

  • 🛗
    Space Elevator Terminal
    Most heavily guarded location in the city. Level 2+ clearance required.
  • 🍺
    Stairway to Heaven
    Bar below the elevator. Best place to find someone with elevator access.
  • ⛪
    Shelter for the Cursed
    Non-profit refuge under threat of eviction by Hughes Industries.
  • ⚡
    Power Relay Station Gamma
    Controls elevator power. Hardened against EMP and physical attack.

Commercial District

Income: Mixed Security: Standard

Commerce, legitimate and otherwise. The Commercial District handles everything the city buys and sells — officially and unofficially. The two systems are deeply integrated.

Key Locations

  • 🏦
    Credit Collective Bank
    Main commercial bank. Their debt enforcement methods are not legal.
  • 🔫
    Gunter's Firearms
    Licensed front with significant grey-market stock in the back.
  • 🕳️
    No Questions Asked
    Permanent black market. Entry point changes every 72 hours.
  • 📊
    DataBroker Exchange
    Information trading floor. Licensed operation and sensitive back room.

Docks

Income: Working Class Security: Port Authority

Everything the city imports and exports passes through the Docks. Legitimate freight through Port Authority terminals. Everything else through the warehouses.

Key Locations

  • ⚓
    The Weary Sailor
    Oldest bar in Hue. Maritime intelligence passes through constantly.
  • 📦
    Strat Warehouses
    Official front for significant smuggling operations.
  • 🌊
    Submarine Bay
    Classified facility. Hughes Industries denies existence.
  • 🐟
    Fisherman's Wharf
    Pre-corporate community. Has resisted seven buyout attempts.

The Underground

Depth: Multiple Levels Jurisdiction: Disputed

Beneath the city's surface lies a vast, unmapped subterranean network. No complete map exists. It is its own city beneath the city.

Key Locations

  • 📡
    The Sanctum
    Psionic dead zone. No Hughes Station emitter signal penetrates here.
  • 💾
    Memory Banks
    Hidden data haven sought by three governments. Not yet found.
  • 👁️
    Whisper Market
    Weekly market dealing exclusively in information.
  • 🦴
    Bone Road
    Deep passage. Parties that follow it entirely do not report back.

Executive

The crown of the City of Hue. Every major corporation maintains a tower here. Private security outnumbers civilians three to one. Streets are clean because they're empty.

🏢 Hughes Industries HQ

Tallest Structure in Hue CORP

The seat of Jonathan Hughes' empire. 92 floors. The upper 20 are sealed to all but Level-3 clearance holders. Hughes' face is projected on the south facade 24 hours a day. A fusion reactor in the sub-basement powers the entire block. Rumour: Floor 87 houses a private tribunal — people enter and do not leave.

⚙️ Prometheus Corp

Military Contracting CORP

Second-tallest tower in the district. Prometheus supplies weapons systems, armored vehicles, and private soldiers to Hughes Industries and the city-state authority. Their lobby is open to the public. Their loading bays, working around the clock, are not.

🧠 NeuralSync Technologies

Augmentation R&D CORP

Cutting-edge neural implant research firm. Publicly traded but majority-owned by Hughes Industries. Their test subjects sign contracts most lawyers would refuse to read. The building smells faintly of antiseptic and burnt silicon.

🏦 VaultBank — Hue Central

Tier-1 Financial Institution CORP

The most secure banking facility in the city. Physical vault six levels underground, reinforced against seismic, EMP, and forced entry. Rumoured to hold holdings for three different governments simultaneously. No branch hours — appointment only.

🥂 Arcadia Club

Elite Members-Only Social Club COMMERCIAL

Invitation only. Members include senior Hughes staff, foreign dignitaries, and the occasional crime boss who has purchased respectability. The wine list is legitimate. The conversations at the back tables are not. No recording devices function inside — jamming is built into the walls.

🌃 The Spire Hotel

Five-Star Executive Lodging COMMERCIAL

The only hotel in the Executive District. 340 rooms, all suites. Corporate clients only — ID and clearance checked at the door. A private helipad on the roof connects directly to the penthouse floors. The concierge has seen things that would end careers.

🛡️ Security Station Alpha

Hughes Private Security HQ CORP

Central command for all private security operations in the Executive District. 200 officers on shift at any time. Armed with corporate-grade weaponry. Authority supersedes city police within district boundaries. They answer only to Hughes Industries.

🚁 Executive Helipad Complex

Private Air Transit Hub SERVICE

Four landing pads for private transport. Connects the Executive District directly to Hughes Station via atmospheric shuttle. Access requires Level 2+ clearance and advance booking. The shuttle runs twice daily.

🗿 Hughes Memorial Plaza

Public Square (Monitored) SERVICE

A wide, clean plaza fronting the Hughes HQ tower. Surveillance density: one camera per 4 square meters. A bronze statue of Jonathan Hughes stands at the center — 12 meters tall. Protests are legally classified as 'atmospheric disturbances' and dispersed within minutes.

⚖️ Meridian Law Chambers

Corporate Legal Authority CORP

Hughes Industries' internal legal arm. Handles everything from contract disputes to internal 'corrections'. Their verdicts carry the force of law within Hughes-owned territory. No appeals process. No public record.

⚕️ ExcelMed Private Clinic

Executive Healthcare SERVICE

Elite medical facility for corporate staff. Full augmentation suite, trauma surgery, gene therapy. Street cost would be prohibitive. Corporate employees receive it as a benefit. No record-keeping on procedures that would embarrass the corporation.

🌿 Hughes Private Gardens

Restricted Green Space SERVICE

A genuine garden with actual trees — a rarity in Hue. Maintained by a dedicated groundskeeping staff of 40. Access restricted to Hughes senior management. The garden contains a secure outdoor meeting area where sensitive conversations occur, safe from electronic surveillance.

🏰 Executive Residences

Corporate Housing — Gated RESIDENTIAL

Six residential towers exclusively for C-suite officers and their families. Biometric entry, private elevators, armed concierge. Residents rarely need to leave the district. Everything is delivered — or is already inside.

🚧 North Gate Security Post

District Entry Control DANGER

The primary entry/exit point for the Executive District. All persons scanned. Vehicles searched. Unauthorized individuals are turned away. A second, unmarked gate exists on the west wall for unlogged movement of cargo and persons.

Commonwealth

The most liveable district in Hue. Functioning utilities, actual parks, and security that responds within minutes. It is aspirational — most of Hue can see it but cannot afford to live here.

🌿 The Garden

Public Park & Green Space SERVICE

The largest green space in the city. Genuine soil, cultivated trees, and maintained footpaths. On clear days you can see the space elevator from the east bench. Families, joggers, and office workers at lunch. A small illegal market operates near the north entrance after dark — the local security post looks away.

🏘️ Commonwealth Tower A

Above-Average Residential RESIDENTIAL

32 floors of well-maintained apartments. Waiting list typically 8 months. Residents receive full utility access, climate control, and ground-floor retail. Management committee handles disputes internally. Quiet building, tight community.

🏘️ Commonwealth Tower B

Above-Average Residential RESIDENTIAL

Slightly newer than Tower A. 28 floors. Houses a number of mid-level corporate employees and professionals. The rooftop communal space has a licensed bar that serves until 02:00. Considered the livelier of the twin towers.

📚 Rowan Academy

Private Secondary School SERVICE

One of only three accredited private schools in Hue. High entry standards, corporate sponsorship from three firms. Alumni go directly to corpo internships. The headteacher has undisclosed connections to Hughes Industries HR.

🍷 The Meridian

Upscale Bar & Restaurant COMMERCIAL

Commonwealth's finest dining. Reservation required two weeks in advance. The kitchen sources actual non-synthesized food where possible. Popular venue for corporate 'informal meetings' — the private dining room is swept for bugs weekly, by both the owner and corporate clients.

🛒 Sunrise Market

Daily Provisions Market COMMERCIAL

Clean, organized market. Stalls selling fresh produce, quality synth-food, and household goods. Unlike the commercial district, prices are fair and stalls are licensed. The market runs 06:00–20:00. A small produce black market operates in the alley behind it.

⚕️ Commonwealth Health Clinic

Licensed Medical Facility SERVICE

Full-service clinic with actual doctors. Walk-in hours, appointment system, pharmaceutical dispensary. Significantly cheaper than ExcelMed across the wall. The waiting room has magazines less than a year old.

🍜 The Jade Terrace

Restaurant & Community Hub COMMERCIAL

Family-run restaurant, three generations deep. Serves fusion cuisine. Popular with long-term residents. The back room hosts community meetings, neighborhood dispute resolution, and the occasional illegal card game for local regulars.

🛡️ Commonwealth Security Post 3

Municipal Security Station SERVICE

Licensed security contractors operating under city mandate. Response time under 4 minutes within district. Officers are paid enough to not take bribes — mostly. The station has a small holding cell and processes minor offenses locally.

💻 The Tech Quarter

Small Business Office Hub COMMERCIAL

Cluster of small-to-medium tech firms, freelance engineers, and independent consultants. Four office buildings sharing a courtyard. Coffee shop on ground floor. Security is self-managed. A few tenants have zero public presence and receive visitors at unusual hours.

🚶 Greenway Pedestrian Zone

Public Transit & Walking Route SERVICE

Elevated walkway connecting Commonwealth to the Central and Executive districts. Lined with automated planters, public seating, and surveillance cameras. Clean and well-lit. Unofficially, it is also the route used by couriers moving goods between districts without surface-level inspection.

Central

The backbone of Hue's working population. Middle-income residential blocks packed tightly together, neighborhood shops, and the constant hum of urban life.

🚊 Central Transit Hub

District Transport Center SERVICE

The main transit node for the city. Connects to all other districts via rail, bus, and automated pod lines. Processes 180,000 passengers daily. The lower level has unofficial luggage storage run by a man named Kev who doesn't own a record book. Lost & Found is a misnomer — things go to Kev.

🏗️ Midtown Residential A

42-Floor Housing Block RESIDENTIAL

Standard mid-income housing. 840 units. Common areas maintained quarterly. The 17th floor laundry room is a known meeting point for the local neighborhood watch — who also run an unlicensed gambling operation on Thursdays.

🏗️ Midtown Residential B

38-Floor Housing Block RESIDENTIAL

Slightly newer than Block A. Integrated with the transit hub's eastern entrance. Ground floor hosts a mix of small shops. Residents of floor 3 have a long-running dispute with management about the ventilation system that predates everyone currently employed there.

🏗️ Midtown Residential C

50-Floor Housing Block RESIDENTIAL

The tallest residential tower in the district. Upper floors have a view over the smog layer on clear days. A functioning residents' association. The building's basement connects — allegedly — to a passage that leads toward the Underground.

🍔 Food Fight

Fast Food Chain — Central Branch COMMERCIAL

Cheapest hot food in the district. Synthburgers, neon sauce, 28 varieties of flavored protein paste. Open 24 hours. The night shift staff have seen things. The booth in the back-left corner is where half the district's informal business gets conducted.

🔧 The Rusty Bolt

Hardware & Repair Shop COMMERCIAL

Multi-generational hardware store. Sells parts, tools, and fabricated components. The owner, a retired mechanic named Silke, can fix almost anything and asks no questions about what it was originally for. Her back-room parts inventory is significantly larger than the shop front suggests.

📖 Hue Public Archive — Branch 7

Public Library & Data Terminal SERVICE

One of the few public data terminals in the city not actively monitored in real-time. The archive holds physical printed records going back to Hue's founding — increasingly rare. The librarian, an elderly orc named Petrus, knows where everything is and will help anyone who is polite.

💉 Medicore Clinic

Budget Healthcare — Walk-In SERVICE

Affordable medical care for the district. Long queues, overworked staff, adequate outcomes. The clinic quietly performs off-book augmentation corrections for people who can't afford official services. Ask for the night doctor.

💾 Neon Pawn

Pawnshop & Second-Hand Goods COMMERCIAL

Buys and sells everything. Electronics, weapons, personal items, data chips of uncertain origin. The owner is deeply uninterested in provenance. A locked case near the back contains items the owner has decided not to sell — the price list for those is different.

🏛️ Central Community Hall

Civic & Event Space SERVICE

Hosts public meetings, community events, and dispute mediation. The hall is ostensibly neutral ground — even gang representatives attend when district-wide issues arise. Booking the hall for a private event is cheap. The calendar is always suspiciously full on the third Tuesday of each month.

🛡️ Security Post 7

Municipal Security — Central SERVICE

Underfunded and understaffed. Four officers covering the whole district. They respond when they can. They have a complicated relationship with the community — some residents see them as allies, others as a threat. They turn a blind eye to a lot because they have to.

🛒 Central Market

Daily Open-Air Market COMMERCIAL

Sprawling daily market filling a three-block area. Legitimate produce, second-hand goods, and clearly illegal items coexist peacefully. The stall owners self-police — the market works because everyone has agreed it works. Outsiders trying to shake down vendors have a habit of leaving the district quickly.

Shantytown

No official maps of Shantytown are accurate. The district grows and shifts — structures built overnight, passages blocked by new construction, entire blocks changing function within weeks.

🪙 Yamai's Pawnshop

Drow Proprietor — No Questions COMMERCIAL

Run by Yamai, a drow who arrived in Shantytown twenty years ago and never left. Buys everything. Sells most of it. Knows the value of things that have no official price. The back room has been used for mediation, hiding, and at least one birth. Yamai's memory for faces is perfect and permanent.

🍺 Broken Dreams Bar

Dive Bar — Information Hub COMMERCIAL

The social center of Shantytown. Cheap synth-ale, no dress code, frequent violence that everyone ignores. Information trades here — who is hiring, who is hunting whom, what moved through the city last night. The bar top has been repaired so many times it is now mostly old repair materials.

🍲 Mother Hen's

Community Kitchen — Free Meals SERVICE

Operating for eleven years on pure donations and stubbornness. Serves 300 meals a day. Run by a collective of Shantytown residents. No records, no questions. An unspoken rule: no violence within sight of Mother Hen's. Everyone enforces this, including the gangs.

⚙️ The Scrapyard

Salvage & Parts Market COMMERCIAL

A vast open salvage market. Industrial machinery, vehicle parts, building materials, and electronic components in various states of function. The 'market' has no fixed prices — everything is negotiated. Three families have divided the yard between them for decades. Outsiders are welcome to buy. Not to loiter.

👊 The Pit

Underground Fighting Ring DANGER

A fighting ring operating in a converted loading bay. No rules, no weight classes, full bets accepted. Runs three nights a week. The pit has its own informal medical staff — two retired field medics who patch fighters for a cut of the winnings.

🌀 The Warrens

Labyrinthine Residential Core RESIDENTIAL

The densest residential zone in Shantytown — perhaps the whole city. Structures three and four stories tall built on top of each other, connected by bridges, rope ladders, and passages that appear and disappear. Residents navigate by instinct. Emergency services have a standing policy of not entering.

⬇️ The Hollow

Underground Access Point DANGER

One of the most well-known surface access points to the Underground. A drop of about 4 meters through a collapsed floor in a derelict structure. The Underground residents below have installed a rope ladder. Traffic goes both ways at all hours.

🩹 Patchwork Medics

Unlicensed Medical Station SERVICE

Three medics with mixed credentials operating out of a converted workshop. No augmentation installs, but trauma care, suture, and pharmaceutical access. They charge what people can pay. Their supply chain for medicine is creative and entirely unregulated.

🗣️ The Assembly Point

Shantytown Governance — Informal SERVICE

An open concrete yard where district-wide decisions are made by the people who live here. No elected officials. No corporate liaison. Decisions are binding by collective agreement and enforced by everyone. Outsiders have tried to co-opt this process. None have succeeded.

🔩 Salvage Row

Strip-and-Sell Operations COMMERCIAL

A long strip of open-front workshops where vehicles, electronics, and structures are disassembled and sold by component. Salvage Row is the endpoint for a significant portion of the city's stolen property pipeline.

🕯️ The Shrine

Community Memorial — Protected Ground SERVICE

A memorial to district residents who died in the Corpo Wars. Maintained communally. Like Mother Hen's, it is protected ground — violence near the Shrine is one of the few acts that will unite all Shantytown factions against a common target.

👁️ The Watchtower

Gang Lookout Post — North Entry DANGER

A makeshift tower at the north entry to Shantytown's inner zone. Manned around the clock. The watchers track who enters, who leaves, and who is carrying what. This information feeds to every gang in the district simultaneously — a rare point of cooperation.

Entertainment

The district that never closes. Every vice has a price, every spectacle available for the right price. The Entertainment District is Hue's pressure valve — and its most profitable square kilometer.

🎰 Lucky Dwarf Casino

Multi-Level Gambling Venue COMMERCIAL

Six floors of licensed gambling. Ground floor: slots, automated games. Floors 2–4: table games, escalating stakes. Floor 5: private rooms for high rollers. Floor 6: does not officially exist. The basement runs an illegal sports book and a numbers operation. The building's security staff have more combat experience than most city police units.

⚔️ Bloodsport Arena

Licensed Combat Venue DANGER

The largest entertainment venue in the district. Capacity 8,000. Hosts sanctioned death matches, augmented fighter bouts, and the Merc Circuit. Corporate-sponsored fighters get entrance music and branded gear. Underground tournament runs parallel — winners are invited. Not all of them come back. All of it is broadcast.

🎵 Club Vertigo

Nightclub — 24hr License COMMERCIAL

Four floors of music, synthesized chemicals, and questionable decisions. Popular with everyone from factory workers to corporate assistants. The VIP section on floor three is technically invitation-only — the invitation is a particular style of wallet.

🏨 The Jackpot Hotel

Casino-Attached Lodging COMMERCIAL

200 rooms attached to the Lucky Dwarf. Hourly rates available, no questions. Discretion is the hotel's primary product. Security footage retention policy: none. Three guests have died in-room in the past year. None of the deaths were investigated.

🀄 Fortune 8

Second-Tier Casino COMMERCIAL

Smaller and louder than Lucky Dwarf. Attracts a rougher clientele and looser enforcement. Fortune 8 is also the main venue for an informal information market — a broker sits at table 14 every night from 22:00, buying and selling actionable intelligence.

💎 The Velvet Syndicate

High-End Members Club COMMERCIAL

The most exclusive venue in the district. Membership by referral only. No visible branding outside — a plain black door. Inside: real spirits, private rooms, a discreet medical service, and a clientele who are never photographed.

🎭 Chromatic Lounge

Art Bar & Live Performance COMMERCIAL

The district's cultural fig leaf. Genuine live music, performance art, and a less aggressive atmosphere. Hides a document forgery operation behind the stage. Artists need supplemental income.

🥊 Rex's Fight Club

Unlicensed Combat Venue DANGER

Everything the Bloodsport Arena is, minus the license and the cameras. Bare knuckle, any method. Rex runs it and personally throws out anyone who brings weapons into the crowd.

🌙 The Afterparty

Late-Night Venue — Opens 03:00 COMMERCIAL

The Entertainment District's final destination. Opens when everything else closes. No cover charge, no dress code, no operating license technically in effect. The Afterparty has been 'about to be shut down' for nine years.

🔬 Chrome & Flesh

Augmentation Parlor — Grey Market SERVICE

Licensed for minor cosmetic augmentation. Performs significantly more that after hours. Popular with fighters and anyone who needs functional combat upgrades without a corporate medical record. Their prices are fair and their rejection rate for complications is low.

🛡️ Entertainment Zone Patrol

Corporate Security Post — Strip CORP

Patrols the Strip primarily to protect business revenue, not civilians. Response time to venue disputes: 90 seconds. Response time to civilian complaints: not measured. They maintain order and are entirely uninterested in what happens in the unlicensed venues.

Industrial

The engine room of Hue. Factories running 24-hour cycles, freight moving constantly, and at the district's center: the base of the Space Elevator.

🛗 Space Elevator — Base Terminal

Orbital Tether — Checkpoint Omega CORP

The most heavily guarded location in the city. The elevator tether anchors here and rises 420km to Hughes Station. Entry requires Hughes Industries Level 2+ clearance, biometric scan, full body search, and a pre-approved manifest. Eight-hour ascent. The terminal processes approximately 40 approved passengers and 200 tonnes of cargo per day.

🏭 Hughes Fabrication Plant 1

Heavy Manufacturing — Weapons Components CORP

Produces weapons systems components for both the city-state authority and external contracts. Three shift rotations. Workers sign NDAs. A persistent underground rumour says the plant's basement produces something the public manifest doesn't mention.

🏭 Hughes Fabrication Plant 2

Biotech Assembly — Augmentation Components CORP

Assembles augmentation components from NeuralSync designs. Output goes primarily to ExcelMed and a classified Hughes Industries supply chain. The workers here have better protective gear than Plant 1. This is noted but not discussed.

💊 Biomed Assembly Complex

Medical Device Manufacturing CORP

Produces hospital-grade equipment and pharmaceutical compounds. Legitimate output. The shipping logs, however, show deliveries to addresses that do not match any registered medical facility. The discrepancy has been raised internally twice. Both times, the person who raised it transferred departments.

🚧 Checkpoint Omega

Elevator Security Command CORP

The security command for the elevator terminal. Staffed by Hughes' most trusted private military contractors. They operate under standing orders that supersede city law. Lethal response is authorized for anyone who breaches the exclusion zone perimeter without clearance.

🍺 Stairway to Heaven

Workers' Bar — Elevator Crew Hangout COMMERCIAL

Named after the elevator it overlooks. Run by a retired cargo operator named Maas. Frequented by elevator technicians, factory workers, and the occasional person trying to bribe one of them. Maas is aware of this last category and accepts a cut.

📦 Freight Depot Alpha

Cargo Staging & Transit Hub SERVICE

Primary cargo staging area for all freight entering and leaving the elevator. The depot processes more cargo than its official manifest suggests — the discrepancy is documented only in the depot manager's private ledger, which she keeps at home.

⚡ Power Relay Station Gamma

District Power Infrastructure CORP

Controls power distribution for the elevator terminal and surrounding industrial zone. Losing this station would shut down the elevator and approximately 60% of the district's manufacturing capacity. The station is hardened against EMP, physical attack, and unauthorized access.

⛪ Shelter for the Cursed

Non-Profit Refuge — Augmented Persons SERVICE

Running on community donations for 14 years. Currently under pressure from Hughes Industries, which wants the land. Three eviction proceedings have been filed and defeated. The fourth is pending. The shelter has no political allies — only a community willing to block the bulldozers.

🏘️ Worker Dormitory Blocks

Factory Staff Housing RESIDENTIAL

Functional, oppressive, owned by Hughes Industries. Workers can deduct rent from wages — in practice this means they can't save. A tenant union has been attempted three times. The third attempt is ongoing.

🔧 The Grease Trap

Worker Bar — Unlicensed COMMERCIAL

An informal bar that has operated out of a converted maintenance bay for eight years. No license. Hughes security has raided it twice. Both times, every worker in the district called in sick the following morning. Hughes decided the raids were not cost-effective.

🏥 Industrial Medical Station

Trauma & Occupational Health SERVICE

Handles factory accidents, machinery injuries, and chemical exposures. Owned by Hughes Industries. Their paperwork systematically understates injury severity to avoid compensation claims. Workers know this. They have no alternative.

Commercial

Commerce, legitimate and otherwise. The Commercial District handles everything the city buys and sells — officially through licensed businesses, unofficially through a parallel economy.

🏦 Credit Collective Bank

Corporate-Backed Financial Institution CORP

The district's main commercial bank. The vault has not been successfully robbed in 30 years — not because of the security, but because whoever has tried has subsequently disappeared without complaint being filed. The loan department's interest rates are legal. The debt enforcement arm's methods are not.

🏦 VaultBank — Commercial Branch

Secure Financial Services CORP

A branch of the Executive District's tier-1 bank, handling commercial-volume transactions. Most of the city's legal money flows through here at some point. The manager is a nervous man who has clearly been told something he wishes he hadn't.

📊 DataBroker Exchange

Information Trading Floor COMMERCIAL

A licensed exchange for commercial data. The back room handles information of a more sensitive variety: corporate secrets, blackmail material, personnel files. Payment in untraceable currencies only.

🔫 Gunter's Firearms

Licensed Weapons Dealer COMMERCIAL

Gunter sells legal weapons and ammunition through the front counter. He sells considerably more through the back. His grey-market stock includes military-surplus equipment, prohibited modifications, and weapons with serial numbers that have undergone a manufacturing defect.

🦾 Cyberware Emporium

Legal Augmentation Sales COMMERCIAL

Sells licensed augmentation hardware. The showroom is bright and legal. The stockroom contains models that have not received city approval — available for 'research purposes'. Customers with the right referral can access a fitting service in the basement.

🛍️ The Neon Bazaar

Permanent Indoor Market COMMERCIAL

A covered market across two floors. 140 stalls selling everything from food to electronics to questionable documents. No stall is officially registered as what it actually sells. The market association self-regulates, handles disputes, and maintains a collective insurance scheme.

🤝 The Broker's Den

Commercial Services Hub COMMERCIAL

A cluster of small offices providing contract negotiation, currency exchange, and identity verification. The identity verification desk accepts payment to verify identities that may not fully correspond to the individual presenting.

💱 Hue Pawn & Trade

Pawnshop & Resale COMMERCIAL

The largest pawn operation in the district. The third floor holds inventory of a quality and provenance that requires specific introductions. The owner, a cold-eyed woman named Devaris, has a memory for valuations that makes people uncomfortable.

🕳️ "No Questions Asked"

Black Market — Location Redacted DANGER

The district's open secret. A permanent black market operation accessible through a referral system that changes its entry point every 72 hours. Sells: prohibited augmentations, military hardware, forged documentation, classified data. The operators have never been raided.

💊 MediMarket

Pharmaceutical Sales — Semi-Licensed COMMERCIAL

Legal pharmaceutical sales alongside a grey-market section for medications that require a prescription most people cannot obtain. Their supplemental stock comes from the Biomed Assembly Complex via a route neither party acknowledges.

🚛 Commercial Transit Depot

Freight Routing Center SERVICE

Coordinates cargo movement between the Commercial District and the rest of the city. The official manifest covers 60% of the volume moving through the depot. The rest is routed through a parallel booking system.

🛡️ Commercial Security — District Post

Joint Corpo-Municipal Operation SERVICE

Funded jointly by Banking Row institutions and the city authority. Their priorities reflect their funding: financial institution security first, public safety second. They are aware of the black market operation. This awareness has a monthly cost, paid in cryptocurrency.

Docks

Everything the city imports and exports passes through the Docks. Legitimate freight through the Port Authority terminals. Everything else through the warehouses that technically only store legitimate freight.

⚓ The Weary Sailor

Dockside Bar & Information Hub COMMERCIAL

The oldest continuously operating bar in Hue. Every piece of maritime intelligence in the city passes through at some point — arrival schedules, manifest discrepancies, which Port Authority officer is covering which gate tonight. The barkeep, a former navigator named Coral, hears everything and sells selectively.

⚓ Port Authority HQ

Maritime Law Enforcement CORP

The official port administration and law enforcement body. Approximately 40% of the staff are uncompromised. The other 60% have standing arrangements with shipping operators, which is known to the 40% and tolerated because the alternative is worse.

📋 Customs Office

Cargo Inspection — Official CORP

Officially processes all inbound cargo. In practice, approves what the Port Authority directs it to approve. The customs officers have a rotation schedule that is posted, unofficially, on three different smugglers' networks 48 hours in advance.

📦 Strat Warehouses — Block A

Freight Storage — Front COMMERCIAL

The official face of Strat's operations. Stores legitimate contracted cargo. The Port Authority inspectors assigned to Strat warehouses have the district's lowest rejection rate and highest overtime hours.

📦 Strat Warehouses — Block B

Freight Storage — Actual DANGER

Adjacent to Block A, connected by a corridor not on the public floor plan. The cargo moved through Block B does not appear on the Port Authority manifest system. Three shifts of workers who do not appear on Strat's official payroll.

🍺 The Salt Bar

Second Dockside Bar COMMERCIAL

Rougher than The Weary Sailor and aware of it. Caters to day-shift dock workers and the type of sailors who cause problems on other ships. Fights are resolved by the bar's owner — a half-orc named Brex — personally and immediately. Brex has never lost.

🚢 Cargo Terminal A

Primary Maritime Freight Handling SERVICE

The main commercial cargo terminal. Three berths for large freight vessels, continuous loading/unloading. The terminal crane operators are among the best-paid workers in the district. They also see everything that gets loaded and unloaded, on every ship, at every hour.

🌊 Submarine Bay — [RESTRICTED]

Classified Maritime Facility DANGER

A concealed marine bay accessible from the ocean side, not visible from the public dock area. Hughes Industries officially denies its existence. Three sources of independent intelligence confirm it houses two submersibles used for below-manifest cargo movement.

🐟 Fisherman's Wharf

Traditional Fishing Operations SERVICE

The oldest part of the Docks — predates the city's corporate construction. A community of fishers who have operated here for generations. They have resisted six buyout attempts by Strat and one by Hughes Industries. They know more about the ocean approaches to the city than anyone.

🏘️ Sailor Residences

Permanent Dockworker Housing RESIDENTIAL

Long-term housing for dock workers and maritime crew. Perpetually damp, adequate maintenance, communal facilities. A tight community with its own informal governance structure. Outsiders moving in are noticed immediately.

⚙️ The Rusty Anchor

Marine Parts & Repair Supply COMMERCIAL

Sells everything needed to maintain a vessel at sea. Parts of uncertain provenance, navigation tools, underwater equipment, and specialized hardware that could serve maritime or non-maritime purposes equally. The catalogue is not posted publicly.

🌊 ~ Ocean Access ~

Maritime Entry — Open Water SERVICE

Open ocean access. City patrol boats operate a loose perimeter. The space between patrol routes is precisely 22 minutes — known to everyone who uses this access point for unofficial purposes.

Underground

Below the city's surface, a second city exists. No complete map exists. Several have been attempted. None agree.

🌀 The Undercurrent

Main Tunnel Junction — Community Hub SERVICE

The central node of Underground life. A massive vaulted space created when three pre-war transit tunnels collapsed into each other. Hosts a permanent market, a community meeting space, and the only reliable source of clean water in the Underground — a filtered tap accessed and sealed into the wall forty years ago.

📡 The Sanctum

Psionic Dead Zone — Protected Refuge SERVICE

A section of the Underground naturally shielded from the Hughes Station Psionic Emitter array. No psionic signals penetrate here — neither surveillance nor influence. The Underground community treats it as sacred ground. People come here to think clearly. The dead zone boundary is marked in paint on the floor.

💀 Dead Man's Junction

Intersection — High Traffic — Dangerous DANGER

Where four major passages meet. High foot traffic makes it a commercial hub and a prime spot for robbery. The junction has its own informal toll system managed by a rotating coalition who take a percentage of commerce in exchange for keeping the major violence down.

⚔️ The Forge

Underground Weapons Workshop COMMERCIAL

A working weapons fabrication shop run by two former factory workers. Produces unlicensed weapons, augmentation components, and custom modifications. No serial numbers. They have a six-month backlog. They do not take rush orders from people they don't know.

👁️ Whisper Market

Underground Information Bazaar COMMERCIAL

A weekly market dealing exclusively in information. Buyers and sellers meet in a designated space. Prices are negotiated by code. The market has its own arbitration system for disputed intelligence. False information sells once; its source never sells again.

💾 Memory Banks

Hidden Data Haven — Classified Archives DANGER

A data storage facility running on salvaged hardware. Stores information that cannot exist on the surface network: suppressed medical records, erased corporate documents, destroyed public archives. Has been sought by Hughes Industries, city authority, and three foreign governments. It has not been found.

🏘️ The Refuge

Permanent Underground Community RESIDENTIAL

A settled community of approximately 400 people who live entirely underground. Built functional housing, a school, a medical station, and a governance structure over two generations. Self-sufficient and extremely cautious about outsiders.

🚊 Old Transit Station Bravo

Pre-War Structure — Repurposed SERVICE

A sealed pre-war transit station reoccupied and repurposed. The old platforms serve as sleeping areas. The transit tunnels connect to passages that surface in every district — the Underground community's primary navigation advantage.

☣️ The Sump

Toxic Lower Level — Avoid DANGER

The lowest accessible level. Flooded with chemical runoff from the Industrial District above. Air quality requires breathing protection. Despite this, at least three separate parties maintain operations here — either because the toxicity is a security feature, or they have nowhere else to go.

🦴 Bone Road

Deep Passage — Unknown Destination DANGER

A long passage named by the first Underground resident to map it — for reasons she documented but the document was lost. Parties that have followed it entirely have not returned to report where it goes. Parties that turned back report only that after two hours, the walls change material.

⬆️ Surface Access Network

Distributed Entry Points — Variable SERVICE

The Underground connects to the surface through approximately 60 access points. Specific locations are not mapped here — they change. Known stable points include The Hollow (Shantytown), basement hatches in Central, a drainage connection near the Docks, and at least two points in Commercial's oldest structures.
