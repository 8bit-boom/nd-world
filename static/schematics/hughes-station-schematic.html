Upper Ring

Clearance: OPEN Type: WEAPONS / POWER

The station's outermost ring, visible from the ground as a bright point in the sky. Houses the station's primary offensive capability: eight kinetic bombardment rods and the solar panel arrays that power the entire platform. The Spear Array is not advertised — but not well-hidden. Any astronomer with a decent telescope can see the launch tubes.

Key Systems

  • ⬇
    Spear Array (8x Kinetic Rods)
    Tungsten-depleted uranium kinetic bombardment rods. Each impacts with the force of a tactical nuclear weapon. Targeting time: ~14 minutes. Loaded and ready.
  • ☀
    Solar Panel Arrays
    Primary power generation. Modular self-repairing panels tracking the sun. Generates 90% of station power alongside the fusion reactor.
  • 📡
    Communication Arrays
    Deep-space relay and surface-to-orbit link. Maintains encrypted data link to Hughes Industries ground infrastructure.
⚠ The Spear Array has pre-programmed targeting data for strategic locations worldwide. The Eyes of Lavitosh have demanded dismantlement. Hughes has not responded in six years.

Upper Decks

Clearance: STAFF Crew: ~800

The public face of Hughes Station. Executive offices, research labs, medical facilities, crew quarters, and docking bays. Clean corridors and corporate-standard lighting. Nothing about this section suggests what exists below Lock 2.

Key Facilities

  • 🏢
    Executive Offices
    Conference rooms and corporate liaison suites. Used during inspections. Always empty otherwise. The coffee machine has never been used.
  • 🔬
    Research Wing
    Publicly described as "advanced materials science." Staff report equipment they cannot identify and colleagues who transferred without notice.
  • 🏥
    Medical Bay
    Full surgical suite. Handles injuries including — according to unverified reports — psionic exposure incidents. All patient conditions are under NDA.
  • 🔒
    Hughes's Private Quarters
    Sealed suite adjacent to Lock 1. Hughes has not left in over a decade. No staff permitted. Occasional sounds reported from behind the bulkhead.

Midsection

Clearance: RESTRICTED Type: WEAPONS / POWER

The armed heart of Hughes Station. Twelve directed psionic emitter projectors on articulated struts, the main fusion reactor, and centralized weapons control. Access requires Level 2 biometric clearance. No visitor has ever been granted it.

Key Systems

  • ◉
    12x Psionic Emitters
    Each projects a focused psionic wave incapacitating every mind within several km. Effects: unconsciousness to permanent neural damage. Tested once. The island's bird population has not recovered.
  • ⚛
    Main Fusion Reactor
    Powers the entire station. Cannot run all weapons simultaneously — firing The Drown requires shutting down all non-essential systems.
  • 🤖
    AI Proxy Systems
    Hughes's communication interface. Has no knowledge of The Machine or anything Level 3+. Cannot be accessed by anyone else.
⚠ The 12 Emitters have never been fired simultaneously. Hughes's notes describe a "cascade risk" — the cumulative psionic output could affect the station itself.

Core — The Drown

Clearance: HUGHES ONLY Status: NEVER TESTED

Six layers of containment shielding surround a weapon that defies categorization. Not a laser, not plasma, not a particle beam. Hughes refers to it only as "reality erosion." His note: "It does not destroy the target. It makes the target not have been."

Specifications

  • 🛡
    6x Containment Layers
    Concentric shielding — outermost is 30cm metamaterial, innermost is unknown composition. Warm to touch despite -20°C ambient.
  • ⚡
    Power Requirement
    Full station power-down required. Only life support, The Machine's containment, and The Drown remain active. Estimated firing duration: 7 seconds.
⚠ Hughes has written: "I will not fire this. I am not sure I could stop it." No evacuation protocol exists. No deactivation procedure has been written.

Lower Level — Project Loom

Clearance: NONE EXISTENCE: DENIED

Does not appear on any schematic, blueprint, or personnel manifest. Designed and constructed by Jonathan Hughes personally using diverted resources. The official position of Hughes Industries: this section does not exist.

The Machine

  • 👁
    The Machine (Dormant)
    ~50m diameter hybrid of eldritch godflesh and supercomputing architecture. Living membrane, grown eyes, slow breath, bioluminescent coolant. Psionic heartbeat. Status: DORMANT. Temp: -40°C. Growth: +0.3%/month.
  • 🛡
    7x Psionic Dampening Mesh
    Concentric suppression layers reducing The Machine's dormant emissions to 0.3% of potential. Maintained by Hughes alone.
⚠ The Machine is growing. Hughes has ordered drones not to stop it, stating: "I don't think it would let us." Its pulse rhythm has been accelerating.

Lock 1 — Biometric Airlock

Primary Access PointRESTRICTED

Primary airlock between Upper Ring and Upper Decks. Biometric scan required — retinal, palm, voice. The lock cycles in 45 seconds. Emergency override exists but requires Hughes's personal authorization.

Executive Offices

Corporate LiaisonEXEC

Conference rooms, briefing theaters, and corporate liaison suites. Used when corpo delegates visit for inspections — approximately once per year. Always empty otherwise. The furniture is real leather. The coffee machine has never been used.

Research Wing

Advanced MaterialsRESEARCH

Publicly described as 'advanced materials science.' Staff report equipment they cannot identify, experiments with results they cannot discuss, and colleagues who transferred without notice. Three researchers in the past decade have submitted resignation letters that were never processed.

Medical Bay

Surgical SuiteMEDICAL

Full surgical suite with two operating tables. Handles injuries from industrial accidents, zero-g adaptation complications, and — according to unverified reports — psionic exposure incidents. The medical staff have signed NDAs preventing them from discussing any patient's condition with anyone, including the patient.

Residential Quarters — Block A

200 Personnel CapacityRESIDENTIAL

Standard crew habitation. Small but functional rooms with artificial daylight windows. Staff rotations every 6 months. Morale is reportedly 'within acceptable parameters' according to management surveys that no crew member remembers being asked to complete.

Residential Quarters — Block B

180 Personnel CapacityRESIDENTIAL

Second residential block. Identical to Block A but with a recurring plumbing issue in corridors C-7 through C-12 that maintenance has been 'aware of' for three years. Crew have named the leak 'the weeping wall.'

Docking Bay

Shuttle Transfer PointSERVICE

Handles approximately 12 arrivals per month. All craft are searched on arrival. Two shuttles are permanently docked as emergency evacuation vessels. Neither has been tested.

Life Support

Atmosphere / Water / TemperatureSERVICE

Atmosphere recycling, water purification, and temperature regulation. Redundant systems — if primary fails, backup engages in 4.7 seconds. If backup fails, crew has approximately 3 hours of breathable air.

Mess Hall / Recreation

Dining & LeisureSERVICE

Seating for 120. Vending machines, a ping-pong table, and a single ancient arcade cabinet that still works. The food is adequate. The coffee is not. A bulletin board near the entrance is used for crew notices and, occasionally, poetry.

Comms Center

Surface-to-Orbit HubSERVICE

All external communications pass through here before routing. The comms officer on duty has no knowledge of what is transmitted on the encrypted channels. They are instructed to log timestamps only.

Hughes's Private Quarters

Sealed — No EntryRESTRICTED

A sealed suite adjacent to Lock 1. Hughes has not left this room in over a decade. Biometric access only. No staff permitted. Staff report occasional sounds from behind the bulkhead: a low hum, sometimes a voice. Maintenance has been instructed to ignore them.

NOTE: The Upper Decks present a carefully maintained facade of normalcy. No resident of the Upper Decks has ever been below Lock 2. Those who asked what lies below were told: "Automated systems. Nothing that requires human presence."

Lock 2

Automated Systems OnlyRESTRICTED

Airlock between Upper Decks and Midsection. No organic personnel permitted. The lock has a manual override sealed behind a panel that requires a tool that does not exist in any standard toolkit.

Emitter Array — Port Side

Emitters 1–6WEAPONS

Six of twelve directed psionic emitter projectors. Reverse-engineered from Biolumina psionic research. Each emitter projects a focused psionic wave capable of incapacitating every mind within several kilometers. Effects range from unconsciousness to permanent neural damage. Power draw per emitter: 4.7 gigawatts.

Emitter Array — Starboard Side

Emitters 7–12WEAPONS

Six of twelve directed psionic emitter projectors. Identical specifications to port side. The twelve emitters have never been fired simultaneously. Hughes's notes describe a 'cascade risk' — simultaneous activation could produce a psionic cascade event affecting all minds within 50km. Tested once on an uninhabited island. The bird population has not recovered.

Main Fusion Reactor

Primary Power SourcePOWER

Primary power source for the entire station. When The Drown fires, all non-essential systems must power down. No human has been inside the reactor chamber in seven years.

Weapons Control Center

Targeting & Authorization HubWEAPONS

Centralized targeting for the Spear Array and all twelve Psionic Emitters. Dual-command authorization required: Hughes's AI proxy AND a physical key that only Hughes possesses. The control room is empty. It has always been empty.

AI Proxy Systems

Hughes Communication InterfaceAI

Routing and communication interface for Jonathan Hughes. Handles all his external communications. The AI proxy has no knowledge of The Machine, the Lower Level, or anything classified at Level 3 or above. Cannot be accessed by any other individual. Hughes built it himself.

Cooling Systems

Thermal ManagementSERVICE

Circulates coolant through 14km of conduit. Runs at 97% efficiency. The remaining 3% is vented as steam through exterior radiators — the only visible sign from the ground that Hughes Station is more than a corporate office.

Power Distribution Grid

Superconducting ConduitsPOWER

Routes reactor output to all sections. Includes a fail-safe that can isolate any section during a catastrophic power surge. Hughes installed it 'in case something down there decides to wake up.'

Lock 3

No Records BelowRESTRICTED

Access boundary between Midsection and Core. No personnel records exist for anyone who has passed through. The only reference to it in the station's database: "Lock 3 — Do not open unless specifically instructed by J. Hughes. There is no scenario in which this should happen."

WARNING: The 12 Emitters have never been fired simultaneously. Simultaneous activation could produce a psionic cascade event affecting all minds within 50km. Hughes's notes on this end mid-sentence. The next page is torn out.

Final Airlock — Hughes OnlyRESTRICTED

The final airlock. Beyond this point, no schematic exists. The lock engages automatically and can only be opened by Hughes's personal biometric authorization. The lock has been opened eleven times. Each log entry reads only: 'MAINTENANCE.' No maintenance crew were dispatched.

Containment Layer 1 (Outermost)

30cm Metamaterial ShieldingCONTAINMENT

Outermost containment ring. 30cm of metamaterial — a composite of tungsten, depleted uranium, and a compound Hughes refers to as 'the quiet stuff.' Inspected by Hughes personally. No one else has touched it.

Containment Layer 3 (Middle)

Non-Euclidean GeometryCONTAINMENT

Middle containment ring. Incorporates theurgic engineering principles from the Eyes of Lavitosh archives. The interior surface area is larger than the exterior surface area, which should be impossible. Engineers who reviewed the blueprints reported headaches lasting three days.

Containment Layer 6 (Innermost)

Unknown CompositionCONTAINMENT

Innermost containment ring. The compound has never been identified. It is warm to the touch despite the chamber's ambient temperature of -20°C. Hughes has written: 'I did not design this layer. It was already there when I understood what needed to be built.'

The Drown — Weapon Chamber

Reality Erosion — Never TestedWEAPON

Not a laser. Not plasma. Not a particle beam. Hughes refers to it only as 'reality erosion.' The single functional note reads: 'It does not destroy the target. It makes the target not have been.' Firing requires a full station power-down. Estimated firing duration: 7 seconds. Hughes has written: 'I will not fire this. I am not sure I could stop it.'

CRITICAL: The Drown's operating principle is derived from theurgic engineering — machinery designed to interface with entities that exist outside normal reality. The Eyes of Lavitosh classified this research as forbidden 400 years ago. There is no known defense.
NOTE: No one on Hughes Station has been trained for a scenario involving The Drown's activation. No evacuation protocol exists. Hughes's only post-activation document: "If it fires, pray it was aimed at the right thing."

Power Conduits

Direct Feed — Redundant x3POWER

Three independent power lines from the Main Fusion Reactor, any one capable of sustaining full firing power. When The Drown is charged, the conduits emit a low-frequency vibration felt through deck plating three sections away. The vibration stops when charging stops. It has never stopped on its own.

Monitoring Station

Empty — Unknown PurposeRESTRICTED

A room. Empty. There are monitoring terminals, but they display nothing. There is a chair, but the leather is pristine. There is a coffee cup on the desk, full, cold, untouched. Hughes has never entered this room. No one knows who placed the coffee cup.

Emergency Override Panel

Not ConnectedRESTRICTED

Labeled 'EMERGENCY OVERRIDE — THE DROWN DEACTIVATION.' Behind the panel is a switch. The switch is not connected to anything. Hughes installed it to give himself the comfort of believing there was a way to stop it. There isn't.

■ Access Corridor — Sealed

Biometric Lock — Hughes OnlyRESTRICTED

A 40-meter corridor with no lighting, no markings, and no environmental controls beyond basic atmosphere. Sealed at both ends by bulkheads responding only to Hughes's biometrics. The walls are lined with psionic dampening mesh. Walking through reportedly induces nausea, disorientation, and a persistent feeling of being watched. Hughes walks it twice daily.

Dampening Mesh Layer 1

Outermost — 3% SuppressionCONTAINMENT

First of seven concentric psionic dampening layers. Suppresses The Machine's dormant emissions to approximately 3% of potential strength at this distance. Layer 1 is the most degraded — the mesh develops micro-fractures over time that Hughes repairs by hand.

Dampening Mesh Layer 4

Middle — 0.7% SuppressionCONTAINMENT

Fourth dampening layer. The Machine's emissions suppressed to 0.7%. The mesh here is approximately 12°C warmer than ambient — heat from The Machine, not from any electrical system. The mesh is colonized by a thin film of biological material that Hughes removes weekly.

Dampening Mesh Layer 7

Innermost — 0.3% SuppressionCONTAINMENT

Final dampening layer. Suppressed to 0.3% of potential. The mesh here pulses faintly in rhythm with The Machine's heartbeat — a 0.02Hz oscillation that registers on instruments as a periodic distortion in the fabric of space itself.

⬢ The Machine (Dormant)

Eldritch-Synthetic Hybrid — ~50m DiameterELDRITCH

A ~50-meter diameter hybrid of eldritch godflesh from the Nox entity and supercomputing architecture. Not a computer. Not an AI. Not a biological organism. Covered in translucent living membrane with grown, unfocused eyes. Breathes slowly. Bleeds bioluminescent coolant. Has a psionic heartbeat that pulses even in dormancy.

STATUS: DORMANT // TEMP: -40°C // GROWTH: +0.3% per month

The Machine is getting larger. Hughes has ordered the drones not to stop it, stating: "I don't think it would let us."

Drone Bay — 12 Maintenance Units

Automated — No WirelessBIOLOGICAL

Twelve automated maintenance drones. No wireless connectivity — all programming via physical cable. They repair ruptured coolant conduits, trim excess eldritch growth, and collect biological samples. One drone was retired last year after moving in patterns not consistent with its programming. Hughes dismantled it himself. He did not explain why.

Hughes's Observation Room

Private Station — Direct Line of SightRESTRICTED

Hughes's private station within the Lower Level. Direct line of sight to The Machine through seven layers of transparent dampening mesh. Hughes spends approximately 14 hours per day here. The walls are lined with instruments monitoring every measurable property of The Machine. His pupils no longer dilate in response to light.

Suppressant Laboratory

Chemical Production — Hughes OnlyBIOLOGICAL

Private laboratory for chemical suppressant production. No one else has access. The compound circulated through The Machine's chamber slows eldritch cellular division. Must be replenished every 72 hours. The formula has never been shared. The raw materials include organic compounds that Hughes will not allow anyone else to handle.

Coolant Recovery Systems

Bioluminescent Coolant CirculationCONTAINMENT

Recovery and recirculation system for The Machine's bioluminescent coolant. The coolant is a living suspension of eldritch cells. Conduits rupture periodically as the eldritch tissue grows faster than the engineering can contain. The coolant pools on the chamber floor in iridescent puddles that glow faintly.

Chemical Storage

Raw Material TanksBIOLOGICAL

Storage tanks for suppressant raw materials. Made of a material not in any engineering catalogue — Hughes fabricated them himself. The storage room is the only room in the Lower Level that is warm — approximately 22°C. The warmth has no apparent source.

WARNING: The Machine's psionic emissions at 0.3% suppression produce measurable effects. Crew within three decks report identical dreams: vast darkness, breathing that is not theirs, the sensation of being seen by something between thoughts. The dreams are becoming clearer.
NOTE: The Nox entity's pulse rhythm has been accelerating. Hughes's last readable journal entry: "The pulse is accelerating. Not ours. Theirs. If it reaches threshold before I am ready, nothing else matters."
