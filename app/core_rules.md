# <a name="cyberpunk-ttrpg-players-guide"></a>Cyberpunk TTRPG – Player’s Guide
A gritty cyberpunk–fantasy system built around **feats**, **psionics**, **corporate oppression**, and **eldritch Yellow corruption**.

-----
## <a name="core-stats"></a>1. Core Stats
There are **8 Stats**, split into Physical and Mental categories.
### <a name="physical-stats"></a>Physical Stats
- **Strength** – Raw power, melee damage, lifting
- **Dexterity** – Speed, agility, ranged accuracy
- **Body** – Toughness, endurance, resistance
- **Perception** – Awareness, accuracy, detection
### <a name="mental-stats"></a>Mental Stats
- **Willpower** – Mental resistance, discipline
- **Intellect** – Knowledge, hacking, analysis
- **Charisma** – Presence, persuasion, intimidation
- **Intuition** – Reflexes, instinct, initiative

**Stat Cap:** 10 (unless modified by feats or narrative effects)

-----
## <a name="derived-attributes"></a>2. Derived Attributes
- **Health (HP)** = Sum of Physical Stats + 10
- **Shock** = Sum of Mental Stats
- **Cyber Adaptivity (CA)** = Willpower + Body
- **Speed** = Dexterity + Intuition
- **Physical Points (PP)** = Sum of Physical Stats
- **Mental Points (MP)** = Sum of Mental Stats

PP and MP are spent to activate feats or to boost rolls.

-----
## <a name="character-creation"></a>3. Character Creation
1. Distribute **20 Stat Points**, using one of the following splits:
   - 15 Physical / 5 Mental
   - 10 Physical / 10 Mental
   - 5 Physical / 15 Mental
1. Gain the following feats:
   - 1 **Race Feat**
   - 1 **Profession Feat**
   - 1 **Common Feat**
   - 1 **Free Feat** (any category you qualify for)
1. Start with:
   - **1 Major Edge**
   - **1 Minor Edge**
-----
## <a name="dice-rolls"></a>4. Dice & Rolls
### <a name="core-roll-formula"></a>Core Roll Formula
**Stat + d10 + PP or MP spent**

- You may spend **up to 2 PP or MP** to boost a roll.
- A roll of **10 on the d10** is a **Critical Success**.
- A roll of **1 on the d10** is an **Automatic Failure**.
### <a name="opposed-rolls"></a>Opposed Rolls
- Example (Combat Hit):
  - **Attacker:** Relevant Stat + d10
  - **Defender:** Intuition + d10

## <a name="combat-structure"></a>6. Combat Structure
### <a name="rounds-actions"></a>Rounds & Actions
- **1 Round = 10 seconds**
- Each round you have:
  - **2 Actions** (attack, move, use feats, etc.)
  - **2 Reactions** (used outside your turn)
### <a name="movement-zones"></a>Movement & Zones
- Terrain is divided into **Zones** (~10 meters / 32 feet diameter)
- You may move freely within your current zone
- Entering a new zone costs **1 Action**
- Initiative = Speed
-----
## <a name="weapon-ranges"></a>7. Weapon Ranges
Each weapon has an optimal range category:

|Weapon Type|Range|
| :- | :- |
|Shotguns|Short|
|Handguns|Medium|
|Assault Rifles|Long|
|Sniper Rifles|Very Long|
|Exotic|Varies|

- Each zone beyond a weapon’s range applies **−2 to hit**
- Sniper rifles suffer penalties in close quarters
- Assault rifles suffer penalties when targets are in melee with you
-----
## <a name="damage-states"></a>8. Damage & States
### <a name="health-shock"></a>Health & Shock
- At **0 Health**: You are dying and incapacitated
- At **0 Shock**: You are unconscious
  - Roll d10 each round:
    - **5+**: regain 1 Shock and wake up
    - **3 failures**: unconscious for 1 hour
-----
## <a name="conditions"></a>9. Conditions
- **Burn** – Take 1d8 damage at end of round
- **Freeze** – Lose 1 Action
- **Toxin** – Roll Body vs Difficulty or take d4 damage (ignores armor)
- **Bleeding** – Take +1d4 damage when damaged
- **Blind** – Fail action unless Perception check succeeds
- **Yellow** – Lose 2 HP and 1 Shock each round; at 0, you are consumed
- **Charm** – Cannot attack non-hostile targets
- **Daze** – Lose 2 Actions next turn
- **Stunned** – No Actions or Reactions
-----
## <a name="rest-recovery"></a>10. Rest & Recovery
- On **Rest**:
  - Restore **½ PP and MP** (rounded down)
  - Restore **all Shock**
- Health from medical treatment is restored **after Rest**
- Yellow Priests bypass normal healing limits
-----
## <a name="progression-xp"></a>11. Progression & XP
- Spend XP to:
  - Buy Feats
  - Upgrade Stats
### <a name="costs"></a>Costs
- **Stat Increase**: New Rank × 2 XP
- **Race Feat**: Rank × 4 XP
- **Profession Feat**: Rank × 4 XP
- **Common Feat**: Rank × 3 XP

To unlock the next Rank of feats, you must buy **2 feats of the current Rank**.

-----
## <a name="edge-system"></a>12. Edge System
- **Minor Edge**: Small narrative or mechanical advantage
- **Major Edge**: Powerful narrative control

Limits: - Max **1 Minor Edges** - Max **1 Major Edge**

The GM may offer **Cutting Edge**: > A drastic narrative change in exchange for **1 XP** to the player(s)

-----
## <a name="cyberpsychosis"></a>13. Cyberpsychosis
If **Cyber Adaptivity (CA) ≤ 0**: - If Physical > Mental: you become a **Cyberpsycho** - If Mental > Physical: your body fails and you die

Cyberpsychosis is permanent unless addressed narratively.

-----
## <a name="hacking-net-risk"></a>14. Hacking & Net Risk
- Failed hacks increase **Net Awareness** by 1
- High awareness draws corporate countermeasures
- **Jack Out (Reflex)**:
  - Immediately disconnect
  - Take **1d4 + 2 Shock**
-----
## <a name="stims-limits"></a>15. Stims & Limits
- You may use **no more than 3 stims per Rest**
-----
*This system is lethal, narrative-driven, and rewards smart play over brute force. Choose your battles. The City is watching.*
